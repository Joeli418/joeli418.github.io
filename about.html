About

Objective

We are a group of tutors who try to work what is best for students. We work hard and co-operatively to prepare every session of tutoring and make sure everyone can learn something new and useful. We strongly encourage hard work and provide directions for people who are determined to thrive.

We also try hard to work ethically, as well as valuing individuals' wellbeing. We try to make sure students feel safe and comfortable to work with us. We provide encouragement to people who need.

How Tutoring Sessions Work?

Private tutoring will be conducted with one of our crews through zoom online. Our tutors will support students and help them with homework if needed. We will assist you throughout your journey of HSC. Private tutoring sessions are flexible and a time that best suits your need can be matched.

Group tutoring will be available once 3 or more students sign up for same lessons. Group tutoring will also be conducted through zoom. Group tutoring sessions holds advantage on interactive learning, group working, and price. A group tutor will make sure their team is able to follow and engage in the content they are learning.
